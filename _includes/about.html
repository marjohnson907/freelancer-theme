About

I learned how to cross stitch, sew, crochet, knit, and other crafts as a child. My mother and grandmother enjoyed participating in these activities and taught them to myself and my sisters. Crafting is a way to express myself creatively and relax while working towards a final goal.

I have many projects that I have completed, but I don't display them at home. They are all just sitting in a box. I have meant to frame them, but it can be expensive and I don't know where I would display them. This website allows me to show off the many hours of work I have spent.
